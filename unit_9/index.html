Our Story

It all began with two grandchildren growing up with a grandmother who loved to cook and bake. The family favorite was grandma's banana bread, which contained lots of chocolate chips and extra love. From there, Justin and John decided to share the experience, warmth, and deliciousness of family recipes and opened this bakery.

Our Mission

We thrive on three values: quality, compassion, and convenience. The utmost quality and compassion carefully crafts our products, and for all around convenience, we deliver an order right to your doorstep!

Our Ingredients

Everyone can say that they took the best ingredients and put it together to make it special. For us, we focus on high-quality, GMO free, and organic to make it the best for our specific products.
